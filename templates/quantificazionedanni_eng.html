Areas of activity Damage evaluation: antitrust and intellectual property

The situations where, for anticompetitive behaviours, it is necessary to quantify the losses suffered by a party are numerous: antitrust violations (the so-called private enforcement in civil seat), unfair competition in its multiple expressions, the possible different industrial property rights violations (brands, patents, industrial secrets).

In each of the a.m. cases the main point is how to build the counter-factual scenario, i.e. to suppose in a reasonable way the incomes possible to reach in lack of the denounced behaviours.
